The Trap

Saturday 4pm. Soccer at 5, party at 6, dinner not sorted, boss messaged. You look at the mrs. She looks at you. "What do you want to do?" That's not a question. That's a transfer. You handed it back. Meanwhile the dinner order closes at 8pm. Two taps on an app. Done in 30 seconds. While you were weighing options, the window got smaller and the mrs made the call again.

The Fix
(Cost of Delay + Time Criticality) ÷ Job Size. Highest score goes first. Now you have a reason to back the call.

Your Move
Soccer first. Window closes in 40 minutes, no flex. Order dinner on the app before you start the car. Two taps, 8pm problem closed. Boss message takes 90 seconds. Handle it while you wait at the first red light. Party logistics sort themselves in the car on the way. Walk out and say: "I've got it hon. Soccer, then the party, dinner's sorted." She doesn't ask how. She just knows you're driving.
