The Trap

You compare salary, title, team size, commute. That's Pull vs Pull — you're weighing two attractions against each other. The real question is whether the original Push has been removed. A new title won't fix a broken culture. You'll be back here in 12 months with less leverage and fewer options.

The Fix
Name the Push first. Then ask: does this offer actually remove it?
Map Your Decision — Start Here
Situation Senior analyst. External offer vs internal pitch.
PUSH "I was told there's no growth here." — does the internal pitch actually change that?
PULL "New role = management title + hire authority." — is this what I was missing?
ANXIOUS "Leaving means rebuilding from scratch." — real risk or amplified discomfort?
HABITS "I know how to win here." — honest advantage or just comfortable?
Your Move
Write down what broke the status quo in one honest sentence. If the internal offer removes that thing, stay. If it doesn't, you already know the answer.
