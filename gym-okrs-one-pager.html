The Trap

No clear outcome. No consistent structure. Training in bursts.

Motivated for a week. Gone for two.

Every miss feels like failure. That feeling kills the next session.

You're not lazy. You're unstructured.

The Fix
One objective.
Three key results.
21 days.
Reflect each Friday.
Your First OKR — Start Here
Objective Get visibly stronger in 21 days
KR1 Train 4x per week (30 mins minimum)
KR2 Hit 100g protein daily
KR3 Add 5kg to one compound lift by Day 21
Your Move
Set your OKR before your next session.
Write the objective.
Write the three numbers that prove you hit it.

You're not starting a routine.
You're building a scoreboard.
