Pro Tools for Life  /  EP04  —  Identity MVP

You're not boring.
You just haven't shipped a hypothesis yet.

The Problem
No opinions because you haven't tested any. No preferences because you haven't stayed in anything long enough to form one. You've been designing before you've shipped.
The Tool
Identity MVP. One domain. One hypothesis. 30 days. "I think I care about ___." You're not committing to a personality. You're running a sprint.
The Loop
Build
Pick one domain. Read it, do it, talk about it. 30 days deep.
→
Measure
One question at day 30: did I lean in or pull back?
→
Learn
Keep it, pivot, or kill it. The next hypothesis follows from the signal.
The Trap

Waiting until you feel ready to pick the domain. That feeling is not wisdom. It's the same blank-page problem in a different costume. Pick anything. The signal from a wrong hypothesis is still signal.

Your Move
Fill the blank. "I think I care about ___." One domain. 30 days. Track one signal: lean in or pull back? That's your first data point. Identity is an accumulation of those, not a revelation.
